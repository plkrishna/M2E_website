Simple Steps

  1. Go to the Signup section
  2. Select Startup if you are looking to set up one, otherwise select Mentor
  3. Enter your primary details and click on "Submit"
  4. An OTP will be sent your phone and email which you have to validate on the next page.
  5. After the validation mention your desired username and password.
  6. Complete your profile by filling the professional details.

After the registration is completed you can go to Login section and login into your account using your username and password.
There are 4 modules provided for an account which will open after you login.

  1. Dashboard: Where you you can check recent feed and recently contacted people.
  2. Profile: Where you can store your personal details and upload your CV/resume
  3. Discussion Forum: Where you can post different questions and read previous discussions
  4. Contacts or Requests: Where you can request for personally contacting mentors through phone or email
